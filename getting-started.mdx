# Getting Started

Begin your journey with Paperguide by setting up your account and exploring key features.

## Account Setup

1. **Sign Up**: Register using your email or social media account.
2. **Profile Configuration**: Customize your profile to get personalized recommendations.

## Exploring Key Features

- **Research Assistant**: Start a new project and let the AI assist you in finding relevant sources.
- **Reference Manager**: Organize your references and citations seamlessly.
