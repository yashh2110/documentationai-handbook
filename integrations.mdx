# Integrations

Paperguide seamlessly integrates with your favorite tools to enhance your research workflow.

## Supported Integrations

- **Microsoft Word**: Import and export documents effortlessly.
- **Google Scholar**: Sync your research library with ease.
- **Citation Styles**: Support for APA, MLA, Chicago, and more.

## Setting Up Integrations

1. Navigate to the **Settings** page.
2. Select **Integrations** and follow the prompts to connect your accounts.
